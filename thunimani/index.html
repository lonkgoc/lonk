Our Insane Origin Saga

The Spark of Madness

Testing the Chaos

Manufacturing? Pure alchemy! In our solar-powered factories (yeah, we're that crazy about green), threads weave into fabrics softer than clouds. Fair wages, happy vibes—no compromises.

Clothes That Defy Reality

Fabric Frenzy

Premium organic cotton (200 GSM for tees, 300 for hoodies), bamboo blends for sweat-wicking magic, recycled polyester for eco-warrior vibes. Anti-pilling, fade-resistant, and so breathable you'll forget you're wearing clothes. Perfect for India's heatwaves or AC chills.

Design Delirium

Oversized dropshoulders for that effortless swag, hidden pockets for secrets, glow-in-dark prints for night raves. Colors? From muted earth tones to neon explosions—mix 'n match like a DJ.

Size Spectrum

XS to 5XL, inclusive AF. Custom fits available—send measurements, we'll craft your personal chaos. No one left behind in the style game.

Care Chaos

Machine wash cold, air dry. Lasts forever if you don't feed it to moths. Pro tip: Iron inside out for print longevity. Easy peasy.

Privacy Policy

We collect personal information like name, email, shipping address, and payment details when you place orders or create an account. We also collect browsing data via cookies to improve your experience.

Your data is used for order processing, shipping, customer support, marketing (with consent), and analytics to improve our services. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We use SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, and regular security audits to protect your data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies (Google Analytics) for performance tracking. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data by contacting support@thunimani.in. You can also opt-out of marketing emails anytime.
